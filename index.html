About Me

Information about me

I'm Kent Wilson

A Personal Trainer & Nutrition Specialist based in Johannesburg, South Africa

I have my BSc in Sports Science from the University of Pretoria and have spent many years in the fitness industry transforming people's bodies and quality of life. This website serves as a platform for me to share my knowledge on fitness and nutrition and to connect with people like you, who are looking to trasform themselves, and need someone to show them exactly how to do it.

Training Services

Strength Training

Whether you are training for a Powerlifting or Strongman Competition or wanting to increase your strength for contact sports, my Stregnth Training will ensure you get progressively stronger each week whilst minimizing the risk of injury.

General Fitness

Working towards the goal of overall health and well-being? My General Fitness diet and training plans will allow you to lead a healthier life style whilst improving your quality of life.

Functional Training

If increased coordination, agility and balance is your goal than functional training is for you. This trainig will increase your fitness level whilst building muscle and losing fat.

Weight Loss

To lose weight you have to only eat healthy foods right? Wrong. My Weight Loss programs include tailor-made diet plans which include all of your favourite foods as well as healthy meals that can be made quickly for those who live life on the go.

Contest Prep

Wanting to step on stage and compete in a bodybuilding show? My Contest Prep plan will help you look your leanest and most muscular on stage. This unique 12 week program also features a peak week protocol which will take your physique to the next level.

Muscle Development

Want to feel confident both in and out of your clothing? My Muscle Development plan helps develop the size and definition of each of your muscles ensuring you develop a well-balanced aethestic physique.
